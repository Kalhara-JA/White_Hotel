About Us
White Hotel is a luxurious hotel located in Sri Lanka and picturesque location surrounded by lush greenery and scenic views. The hotel boasts a contemporary and elegant design with a predominantly white color scheme, creating a serene and calming atmosphere. The rooms are spacious, well-appointed, and designed with comfort in mind, providing guests with a relaxing retreat. The hotel offers a wide range of amenities, including a spa, fitness center, swimming pool, and restaurant serving delicious local and international cuisine. The staff at White Hotel are friendly and attentive, ensuring that guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Whether you are looking for a romantic getaway or a peaceful retreat, White Hotel provides the perfect escape.
